NIA INTRO

Introduction to Infosys Nia:

Infosys Nia is a knowledge-based, Integrated, Open AI Software Platform for Enterprise that brings machine learning together with the deep knowledge of an organization to drive automation and innovation thus enabling businesses to continuously reinvent their system landscapes.

Nia ingests the data from various sources of truth for example health metrics, logs, tickets and so on in real time or in a batch mode, makes sense of them and determines the actions to be performed to meet the business SLAs and performs them automatically with appropriate human interventions as needed.

As Nia Automates things, Captures Knowledge, Processes information, Analyzes on its own, does self-healing - as a result Cost of maintenance of application and infrastructure go down dramatically.

Nia provides a solution for end-to-end automation of ticket resolution and other such IT Service Management activities through robotic automation.

It helps to codify and capture the knowledge that sits inside the system and process across generation of people and technology, thus improves business process , makes free from individual dependency

Nia uses several powerful technologies:

Using Ontology, Nia does deep learning by capturing knowledge base inside a system like complex IT systems

Using Natural language processing, Nia understands documentation, understands source code, captures process details etc

Uses Probabilistic method(Bayesian n/w) to diagnose the behavior of the systems and resolves issues

Nia’s Persona Based Dashboards:

Nia allows you to configure various persona based dashboards reflecting the context of the users and their role (persona) in order to amplify their potential. Some of the common views are:

Business Manager View: Summary dashboard of business process overview and related operational and strategic insights actionable at a business process level.

IT Manager View: Operational and Strategic dashboards critical for an IT manager. Examples of insights include task assignment, automation dashboard, root cause analysis, process mining.

Engineer View: Operational as well as strategic insights providing L3 Engineers a 360 Degree view of their IT landscape, assist with root cause analysis, apply knowledge models to enable code impact analysis.
